(Good) design is hard
Good design is hard. Even if you have access to designers I would still recommend using a UI library.

Bootstrap

Material Design

Tailwind CSS

Semantic UI

Material UI is great. Google has made a spectacular job with their styleguide and there are good wrappers for react.

If you want the most flexibility just go with bootstrap. It is the most reliable, has the most users and has the most 3rd party support.

Tailwind is my little secret insider tip. It looks great and the authors are the writers of the book "Refactoring UI" which I wholeheartedly recommend for anyone who is trying to level up in application design.

Semantic UI works really good with React and that is why is my favourite one so far.
